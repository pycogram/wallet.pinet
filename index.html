Unlock Pi Wallet

As a non-custodial wallet, your wallet passphrase is exclusively accessed only to you. Recovery of passphrase is currently impossible.

Lost your passphrase? You can create a new wallet,  but all your π in your previous wallet will be inaccessible.

Download Pi Browser
The Pi Browser is the gateway to Pi’s Web3 app ecosystem where Pioneers engage with apps using their Pi account and transact with their Pi Wallet - it’s the sole platform for securely entering Pi Wallet passphrases.
